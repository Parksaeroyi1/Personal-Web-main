Biography

Russell Llantino (born October 13, 1993), better known by his stage name RUSSELL!

and formerly known as D-Pryde, is a Canadian rapper hailing from Brampton, Ontario...

He is of Filipino and Spanish descent.

Three years after his birth, his father walked out on the family, and was never seen again.
